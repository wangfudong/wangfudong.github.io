Short Bio

I am a PhD candidate at Wuhan University majoring in 3D computer vision under the supervision of Prof. Liangpei Zhang and Gui-Song Xia in CAPTAIN group. Currently, I am working with Prof. Tianfu Wu at NC State University as a visiting PhD student. My research interests include line segment detection and its applications in 3D computer vision.
